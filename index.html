Fort Lauderdale Pool Care

Fort Lauderdale Pool Care provides the area’s premier pool care, maintenance, and pool solutions. Whether you want to create pool habits to keep your new pool clean, or you’re in the market for a professional team that can help you maintain your existing pool – you’ve come to the right place! We operate with a team of qualified pool technicians, who are certified to assist you with all things pool-related, including pool care and maintenance, acid washing and deep cleaning, as well as repairs, replacements, and resurfacing or restoration. Our unique range of quality pool care services was designed with the Fort Lauderdale community in mind. We know that many residents in the area have pools to take care of, and we want to make their job easier by offering our services that are brought straight to your doorstep. We offer professional pool care and maintenance, pump, motor, and filter repair services, as well as acid washing and leak detection.
